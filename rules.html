GUILD RULES
    Wersja Polska
  • 02.07.2020

    - Be nice to the players you play with.

    - Do not use curses or offensive words in the guild chat.

    - Do not provoke other players.

    - You will be kicked out of the guild if you are inactive.

    - If you are planning on taking a break from playing - report this fact to one of the leaders.

    - Being on Discord is mandatory during events organized by the guild.

    - Support your guild, e.g. by participating in guild missions. (Sunday 5:00 PM UTC).

    - If you notice a member behaving badly, report it to one of the leaders.

    - All decisions regarding the development of the guild and its members are made by all guild leaders.

    - Follow the rules and listen to the guild leaders.

    - If you have logged in to the game, please let us know by simply saying hello in the guild chat!

    - The administration reserves the right to change the rules at any time.

    - The administration can punish a person even if he did not break the rules mentioned above, but his behavior or deeds have been found to be inappropriate.
