GUILD MISSIONS
Guild missions are the ones you do in a group of guild friends.
They are divided into: Bounty, Trek, Rush, Challenge and Puzzle


Bounty – NPC on the map to be found and defeated.
Trek – A point on the map that you need to find and interact with.
Rush – A race that must be completed in the designated time. (15 laps)
Challenge – A more difficult task that must be completed. For 6-10 players.
Puzzle – Puzzles that must be completed in the designated time. For more players.
