Wersja Polska
Hello! Please check the information below, before joining our guild.

We are a guild focused mainly on pve, with a slight admixture of wvw.
FTO uses discord and it is needed, if the event
we are together at requires communication (at least listening).
All the events we organize are announced on discord and / or our web page.


I'm a new player. Can I join?

Yes, of course! Our guild supports new players. We help with crafting,
eq and build selection, we answer questions etc.


I'd like to start raiding.

No problem! The FTO organizes raid trainings.
Just follow our events and you will surely find something for yourself!


I'm not a very active player. Is this a problem?

It's not a problem!
If you are able to log in at least once every 3 weeks,
and occasionally participate in events, you will not get a kick.
Our guild is kicking its members after 3 weeks of inactivity.
Of course, you can always come back to us.


I don't have / don't want to use Discord.

You can still join our guild, however you will be excluded from some events,
organized by the guild, e.g. raids or guild missions.
If your reason why you can't use voice chat is more complex,
talk to one of the leaders and you will surely get along somehow.


Are there any rules that should be followed in the guild?

Yes! We have a set of rules that everyone must accept
if they want to join us and observe. You will find them HERE!
Acceptance of the regulations is the basis for a successful recruitment process.


I have read everything and would like to join you!

Great! Let one of the guild leaders know by /w or in-game e-mail, briefly write something about yourself,
and let him know that you accept our regulations! The rest of the recruitment will take place
through an individual interview.
